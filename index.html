Child Safety Standards

doit - The easy event planner is committed to providing a safe environment for all users, including minors. This document outlines our policies and procedures for ensuring child safety within our application.

Content Moderation

doit employs a private-by-default social model that inherently reduces risks to minors:

  • There are no public posts or public content feeds in the application
  • All user interactions require explicit friend connections
  • Users must accept friend requests before any communication or content sharing can occur
  • This closed network approach significantly reduces exposure to harmful content from unknown users
  • We review reported content within 24 hours and take appropriate action based on our terms of service

Reporting Inappropriate Content

Users can report child safety concerns and inappropriate content:

  • Email reporting: All content and safety concerns can be reported directly to our dedicated email: doit.app.contact@gmail.com
  • All reports are reviewed within 24 hours by our team
  • Severe violations, especially those involving child safety, are prioritized and addressed immediately

CSAM Prevention and Response

doit maintains a zero-tolerance policy for Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and related content:

  • We immediately review all reports related to potential CSAM
  • Any confirmed CSAM is removed, the account is terminated, and the case is reported to the appropriate national authorities in compliance with local laws
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations related to CSAM
  • Our closed network system (requiring friend approval) significantly reduces the risk of CSAM distribution
  • We maintain records of all CSAM reports and actions taken in accordance with legal requirements

Age Considerations

While our app does not currently implement technical age verification, we are committed to maintaining an age-appropriate environment:

  • Our Terms of Service prohibit use by individuals under 13 years of age
  • We respond promptly to reports of underage users
  • Parents or guardians can report underage accounts to doit.app.contact@gmail.com
  • We remove accounts confirmed to belong to users under 13 years of age
  • We plan to implement additional age verification measures in future updates

Response to Safety Concerns

We maintain the following response timeline for safety issues:

  • CSAM and immediate safety threats: Addressed within 24 hours
  • Harassment and bullying reports: Reviewed within 48 hours
  • Other safety concerns: Addressed within 72 hours
  • Users will receive confirmation when their report has been received and notification of any actions taken

Contact Information

For any child safety concerns or questions about our policies:

  • Email: doit.app.contact@gmail.com
  • Response time: Within 24 hours for urgent safety concerns
  • This contact point is maintained and monitored continuously
